A

Does continuity show up?

Look for role clarity, early communication, and organized possessions before individual production. Those are the clearest signs that a year inside the program is carrying forward.

B

Can Peat enter the structure quickly?

The question is not whether he produces a highlight. It is whether Phoenix can place him in roles that reveal his strength and processing without making him carry the evaluation environment.

C

Which big controls the environment?

Phoenix lists Khaman Maluach at 7-foot-2, 250 pounds; Portland lists Yang Hansen at 7-foot-2, 270. The useful comparison is not touches. It is screening, positioning, rim presence, and possession discipline.
