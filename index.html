Phoenix / Friday

Beat Portland 81-79 after scoring 24 points before halftime. The Suns produced 57 points after the break, but finished with 18 assists and 20 turnovers.

New Orleans / Saturday

Beat Charlotte 95-91 after trailing 56-45 at halftime. The Pelicans closed with a 30-15 fourth quarter and enter Sunday 1-1.

01 / Set the pace

Can Phoenix run better offense before halftime? The Suns needed a 31-point third quarter after scoring only 24 points in the first half.

02 / Value the ball

Can the Suns move the ball without turning it over? Good passing matters only if the possession ends with a shot.

03 / Win the paint

Can Phoenix control the game inside? Portland outrebounded the Suns 49-44 and scored two more points in the paint.

Local media

John Voita called Koa Peat's first Suns impression "hard to ignore." He highlighted Peat's confidence and physical play while warning against drawing big conclusions from one game.

Fan conversation

Comments in two r/suns threads were mostly positive about the young players' strength and improvement. The most common concerns were shooting and turnovers. These comments are a small sample, not a poll.

AVC interpretation

Game 2 is the check on Game 1. The next test is whether the physical play continues while the team runs better offense.
